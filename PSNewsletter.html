What’s New?
Let’s Talk Attribution
Aligning on how marketing will be measured with the Finance and Revenue orgs is critical to determining how we set marketing OKR’s and allocate our marketing budget across departments. Since the Vista acquisition, we’ve simplified our model to be centered around two metrics:
  1. Marketing Origination refers to the pipeline and billings that marketing delivered to the revenue org. When someone engages with our marketing activities and a rep follows-up with the person because of that activity we categorize that as ‘origination’. We also consider anyone engaging with us as a B2C buyer to be originated from marketing.
  2. Marketing Influence refers to the pipeline and billings that marketing supports prior to and throughout a sales cycle. There are a variety of activities and content that we use to support the revenue org on closing opportunities that they’ve been pursuing for some time. Our impact on those existing pursuits is how we would measure ‘influence’.
